About Bite Club

Join us on Thursdays during lunch to fill your brains with knowledge and fill your stomachs with good food. Learn how to enjoy food while staying healthy.

Our goal is to focus on the ingredients within the foods that students consume; the origins of the things we eat. In addition, we will learn how certain food selections impact our personal health as well as the health of the global community. Ultimately, our hope is to introduce you to more healthy, sustainable, tasty foods that could be easily implemented into your daily lives.

03.23.2015 | To GMO or not to GMO?

Hello Hungry Munchkins!

What is non-GMO food? Why do companies seem to be pushing for consumers to buy it?

First of all, GMO stand for Genetically Modified Organisms. Some foods have been genetically modified to accommodate the demand for a plethora of reasons such as more efficient production or longer shelf lives. The primary argument against the production of GMO foods is the unnatural manner in which the foods are created as well as the unknown potential effects of consuming genetically modified food.

During our meeting, we had a riveting discussion among our members about the pros and cons of GMO foods whilst snacking on both non-GMO and GMO foods.

The conclusion we reached was that there were pros and cons to both sides but genetically modifying food does lead to more efficient production of food which is necessary to feed the ever expanding population.
